ABOUT US

The company came into existence on 11 February 2020 as an online space to make buying and selling easier for the buyers and farmers respectively. The company focuses on the direct buying and selling of goods so that there is no excess money wasted during the selling process. We believe in helping the farmers, the buyers and the companies that want to sell items, in saving money and also providing an online platform where all this exchange is possible.
